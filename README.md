# Global-Airbnb-Performance-Dashboard

# Project Overview

This project analyzes global Airbnb listing data to uncover insights about market distribution, pricing patterns, growth trends, and customer ratings across major cities.

Using Power BI, I built an interactive dashboard that provides a comprehensive view of Airbnb’s global performance. The dashboard helps users explore trends in listings, understand city-level market share, evaluate pricing differences across property types, and analyze customer ratings.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate data visualization, business analysis, and storytelling skills using Power BI.

# Dashboard Preview

The dashboard is divided into three main sections:

# 1️⃣ Overview Page
Provides a high-level summary of the Airbnb ecosystem including:

Total Listings: 279K+

Cities Covered: 10

Hosts: 182K+

Property Types: 144

Reviews: 5.3M+

Key Insights:

Airbnb experienced rapid growth between 2010 and 2016

Listings peaked before declining during the COVID-19 pandemic

Entire home listings dominate the platform compared to other room types.

# 2️⃣ Market Share & Ratings Analysis

This page analyzes the market share of Airbnb listings by city.

Key Insights:

Paris, New York City, and Sydney account for a significant portion of listings.
These cities also generate a large percentage of total reviews.
Hotel rooms are the most expensive property type, followed by entire homes.
The dashboard also includes a ratings heatmap, which evaluates cities based on:

Accuracy

Cleanliness
Communication
Location
Value

Key Insights:

Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro have the highest overall ratings.

London and Istanbul score slightly lower in cleanliness and value.

# 3️⃣ Ratings Comparison

This section compares average city ratings and helps identify:
Cities with the best customer experience
Cities needing improvement in service quality
Differences in rating distribution across cities.

# Key Business Insights

Airbnb experienced rapid expansion from 2010–2016.
Paris, NYC, and Sydney dominate the global listing market.
Hotel rooms are priced significantly higher than private or shared rooms.
Mexico City and Rio have the highest guest satisfaction ratings.
Cleanliness and value-for-money are key drivers of overall ratings.
